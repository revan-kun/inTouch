Is a social networking service, that helps you to maintain communication with your teammates.

Users must register before using the site, after which they may create a personal profile and update it with additional information, add their photos, lists of personal skills, contact information, etc.

  • Every user is able to rate other members of InTouch with like system.
  • User with manager role can create projects, update it and add users to the projects.
  • Advanced search system allows to find other members or projects with specified qualifications or parameters accordingly.
  • Develop and be informed of your co-workers progress. Keep track of interesting projects and take part in their implementation.
  • Constantly upgrade your profile with your latest skills or achievements and let the others know how great you are ;)
